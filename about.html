Short
Biography

I received B.Sc degree of Computer Science in 2012 at Gadjah Mada University, Yogyakarta Indonesia. During B.Sc., I worked as web developer at PT. Gamatechno , a software company in Yogyakarta, Indonesia, being an IT Consultant at Sleman's Local Government, a lecture assistant at Computer Science and Electronics Department as well as Vocational School at Gadjah Mada University. After receiving M.Cs of Computer Science at the same university, I had been working as a lecturer at Computer Science/Informatics Diponegoro University, Semarang Indonesia.

Currently, I am working as a PhD student at the University of Vienna (Uni Wien), and a Project Assistant at the Vienna University of Technology (TU Wien). My research focus including Linked Data, Interoperability and Cybersecurity.
